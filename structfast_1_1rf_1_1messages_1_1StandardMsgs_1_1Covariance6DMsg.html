Detailed Description

6D Covariance

Row-major representation of the 6x6 covariance matrix The orientation parameters use a fixed-axis representation. In order, the parameters are: (X1,X2,X3,X4,X5,X6)

Diagonal elemnts are treated as follows: >0: Valid Covariance 0: Undefined/Not Supported <0: Unknown Covariance.
